Process Model and Analysis for Canopy Growth Corporation
Introduction

Current Process Flow of Manufacturing Cannabis Edibles

Canopy Growth Corporation is a leading cannabis company that manufactures a wide range of products, including cannabis edibles. The current process flow for manufacturing cannabis edibles at Canopy Growth Corporation is outlined below:

  1. Raw Material Sourcing: Procurement of high-quality cannabis and other ingredients.
  2. Quality Check: All raw materials undergo rigorous quality checks.
  3. Material Storage: Storage of quality-approved materials.
  4. Production Planning: Planning the production schedule and resource allocation.
  5. Manufacturing: Actual production of cannabis edibles.
  6. Quality Assurance: Quality checks for the finished products.
  7. Packaging: Packaging the quality-approved edibles.
  8. Storage: Storing the packaged products in a controlled environment.
  9. Distribution: Distributing the products to various retail outlets.
  10. Retail: The final products are sold to consumers.

Shortcomings:

  • Lack of automation in quality checks.
  • Inefficient resource allocation during production planning.
  • Limited storage capacity affecting production scale.

Proposed Business Model

The proposed business model aims to leverage Canopy's strong consumer insights and the changing regulatory landscape in the U.S. With the U.S. moving towards reclassifying cannabis as a lower-risk substance, there is an opportunity for market expansion. The new model proposes diversifying the product range to include more wellness and skincare products. Additionally, Canopy should focus on strengthening its corporate citizenship initiatives to align with its mission of using cannabis for positive change.
