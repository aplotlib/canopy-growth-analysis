Process Model and Analysis for Canopy Growth Corporation
Introduction

Canopy Growth Corporation is a leading cannabis company that focuses on a wide range of products including cannabis flower, vapes, edibles, and more. One area that requires improvement is the manufacturing process of their edible products. The current process flow has several bottlenecks and inefficiencies that need to be addressed.

Proposed Business Model

A new business model is proposed to rectify the shortcomings of the current manufacturing process. The new model aims to streamline operations, reduce waste, and improve overall efficiency.
